Welcome to King Faisal Hospital Rwanda

King Faisal Hospital Rwanda is a multi-specialty quaternary Hospital with a mandate to provide specialized health care in East and Central Africa. For the last 22 years, we have concentrated on clinical excellence, efficiency, and quality in health service delivery.

With our state-of-the-art amenities, equipment, and well-trained staff, we are committed to delivering accessible, affordable, and highest-quality care to our patients.
